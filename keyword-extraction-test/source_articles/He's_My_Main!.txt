'''''He's My Man!''''' (Spanish:'''''¡Es mi hombre!''''') is a 1966 Spanish [[comedy film]] directed by [[Rafael Gil]] and starring [[José Luis López Vázquez]], [[Soledad Miranda]] and [[Mercedes Vecino]].<ref>Bentley p.69</ref>
