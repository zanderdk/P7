The '''Spanish River''' is a river of [[Jamaica]]. It flows from south to north, the height of its delta is 0-40m. The Spring Garden, near the Spanish River, was one of the earliest sugar plantations in Jamaica.<ref>[http://www.discoverjamaica.com/gleaner/discover/tour_ja/tour19.htm Discover Jamaica]</ref>
